About Me   

       My research is focused on developing and utilizing mathematical models to inform ecological management and conservation. In graduate school I have worked on projects ranging from optimal spatial fisheries management to Bayesian survival modeling. Broadly, I am interested in mathematical topics such as optimization, dynamical systems, stochastic modeling, and Bayesian statistics.
